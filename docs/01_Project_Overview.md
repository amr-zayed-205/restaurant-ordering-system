# 2. Project Vision

## Problem Statement

Many small and medium restaurants still receive customer orders manually through WhatsApp or phone calls.

This creates several problems:

- Customers spend time asking about prices and menu items.
- Menus are often shared as images or PDF files, making them difficult to browse.
- Customer service repeatedly answers the same questions.
- Customer information is collected manually every time.
- Some customers leave before completing their orders.

---

## Solution

Build a simple and modern restaurant ordering website that allows customers to:

- Browse the restaurant menu.
- View meal images and prices.
- Add meals to a shopping cart.
- Enter their information once.
- Send the complete order directly to the restaurant via WhatsApp.

The system aims to improve the customer experience while reducing the workload on restaurant staff.
